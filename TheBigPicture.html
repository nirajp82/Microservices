Microservice architecture – a variant of the service-oriented architecture (SOA) structural style – that structures application as a collection of loosely coupled services that are Highly maintainable and testable Loosely coupled Independently deployable Organized around business capabilities Owned by a small team The microservice architecture enables the rapid, frequent and reliable delivery of large, complex applications. It also enables an organization to evolve its technology stack.
